The Mote In God's Eye book review

Published in 1974, The Mote In God’s Eye was a runaway success. It fused the space opera of Star Trek, the world-building of Dune, and the militaristic elements of Starship Troopers. Unlike Star Trek, Dune and Starship Troopers, The Mote In God’s Eye shows its age. Race still divides people.
